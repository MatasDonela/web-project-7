About Us

Welcome to Moovie's Theatre, Iceland’s coziest and most charming movie theatre, right here in Reykjavík! Since 2005, we've been bringing a unique cinematic experience to film lovers across the country—complete with our beloved mascot, Moovie the Cow.

Moovie's Theatre isn’t just any cinema. It’s where stories come to life amidst warm, rustic decor inspired by Iceland’s natural beauty—and where Moovie, our friendly cow mascot, adds a touch of whimsy and fun to every visit.

Our state-of-the-art screens and sound systems deliver crystal-clear visuals and immersive audio for every blockbuster, indie flick, and Icelandic gem we show. Plus, we serve local snacks (including some delicious Icelandic dairy treats that Moovie personally endorses).

Whether you’re here for family movie nights, midnight premieres, or our famous Moovie-themed events, you’ll find a welcoming atmosphere that celebrates film, community, and a little bit of Icelandic magic.

Come say hi to Moovie, grab some popcorn, and enjoy the show. At Moovie’s Theatre, we don’t just watch movies — we celebrate them.
